Privacy Policy — Subs-Please

Effective date: 2025-08-15

Last updated: 2025-08-15

SUBS-PLEASE ("we", "us", "our") provides the Subs-Please Chrome extension ("Extension") that captures audio from a user-selected browser tab to provide live transcription and optional translation.

1) Who we are

SUBS-PLEASE ("we", "us", "our") provides the Subs-Please Chrome extension ("Extension") that captures audio from a user-selected browser tab to provide live transcription and optional translation.

2) Single purpose

The Extension’s single purpose is to capture audio from the active tab and display live subtitles (and optional translation) as an on-page overlay.

3) Data we collect

a) Account and identity (PII)

  • Google account basic profile and email address obtained via Chrome Identity OAuth ("email", "profile" scopes).
  • Purpose: authentication, entitlement/subscription management, and personalization.

b) Authentication information

  • Application session token (JWT) issued by our backend after OAuth code exchange.
  • Purpose: authorize API requests to our services.
  • Storage (device): chrome.storage.local key lt_jwt and optional lt_user

c) Payment/subscription metadata

  • Subscription status and checkout session identifiers (no credit card numbers handled by the Extension).
  • Purpose: enable paid features and billing.
  • Flow: created by backend when you click Upgrade in the popup.

d) Content processed to provide features

  • Tab audio stream selected by you and the resulting transcripts/translations.
  • Purpose: real-time transcription/translation. Audio is captured only while you explicitly start capture and is limited to the chosen tab.

e) Device/technical data (limited)

  • Standard network metadata (e.g., IP address, user agent) processed by our backend and service providers as part of secure HTTPS requests.

We do not collect:

  • Health information, precise location (GPS), keystrokes/mouse/scroll behavior, or full browsing history.
  • Page text/DOM content beyond rendering our overlay UI. Note: audio from the tab may include spoken personal communications.

4) How we use data

  • To authenticate you and maintain a session.
  • To provide live transcription/translation from the selected tab’s audio.
  • To manage subscriptions and payments.
  • To ensure reliability, detect abuse, and improve service (aggregated, minimal telemetry if used).

5) Storage and retention

On your device:

  • chrome.storage.local: lt_jwt, lt_user; localStorage: sourceLang, targetLang.
  • You can clear these via browser settings or by uninstalling the Extension.

On our servers:

  • Account and subscription records retained as required to operate the service and comply with legal/financial obligations.
  • Audio/transcripts are processed to deliver features and are not retained by default beyond what is necessary for processing. If retention is ever introduced (e.g., user-enabled saving), we will update this policy.

6) Data sharing and processors

We do not sell your data.

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate the Extension:

  • Google (OAuth via Chrome Identity) for sign-in.
  • Payment processor (e.g., Stripe) for checkout and subscription management.
  • Speech-to-text and translation providers to process your tab audio and generate subtitles.

All processors are bound by contracts and receive only the minimum data necessary.

7) Permissions and how they’re used

  • activeTab/tabs: Determine the current active tab and open checkout when you click Upgrade.
  • tabCapture: Capture the active tab’s audio when you click Start.
  • scripting/host permissions: Insert the overlay UI script to display subtitles on the selected page.
  • offscreen: Keep the audio pipeline running if the popup closes (no visible UI).
  • storage: Store JWT and language preferences.
  • identity: Sign in with Google via Chrome Identity OAuth.

8) Security

  • Transport security: HTTPS/TLS for all network communication.
  • Access control: least-privilege design, user-initiated capture only.
  • On-device data limited to session token and preferences.

9) Your choices and rights

  • Start/Stop: You choose when to start/stop capture; no background recording.
  • Sign out: Clear data via chrome://extensions > Details > Clear data or remove the Extension.
  • Access/Deletion: Contact cemilenfal@gmail.com to request access to or deletion of your account data.
  • Region-specific rights (e.g., GDPR/CCPA): We honor applicable rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing.

10) Children’s privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11) International transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your own. We use appropriate safeguards for such transfers where required by law.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices or legal requirements change. Material changes will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.

13) Contact

Questions or requests: cemilenfal@gmail.com
